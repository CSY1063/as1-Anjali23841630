My Site Development Experience

Throughout the process of developing my portfolio website, I've encountered various challenges and triumphs. Learning the basics of web development, coding, design, and debugging has been an enlightening journey.

One of the major ups I experienced was grasping the fundamentals of HTML and CSS, enabling me to build the structure and style of my website effectively. However, I also faced downs such as debugging errors in my code and struggling with responsive design.

Reflecting on my module experience, I've gained valuable insights into the importance of user interface (UI) design. I focused on creating a clean and intuitive design for my site, with careful consideration of font choices, colors, and layout.

I drew inspiration from various websites and design resources, such as Dribbble and Behance, to inform my design decisions. By studying successful websites, I gained insights into effective design principles and applied them to my own project.
